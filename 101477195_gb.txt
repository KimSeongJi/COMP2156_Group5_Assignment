George Brown College is a public institution for applied arts and technology in Toronto(ON), Canada founded in 1967.
George Brown College offers around 170 programs across various platforms such as hospitality, health sciences, technology, etc.
This college consists of 3 main campuses; waterfront, St. James and Casa Loma. 
The college provides various support services for students, including academic advising, counseling, tutoring, and career services.
The college has received numerous awards and accolades for its academic excellence, innovation, and commitment to student success.
